#  Swiggy Sales Dashboard Analysis in Excel

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built to analyze business performance for Swiggy. The dashboard delivers actionable insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Business Problem

In a fast-paced food delivery ecosystem, understanding **what drives revenue and customer engagement** is critical. The business needed a solution to:

* Identify high-performing cities and states
* Analyze customer ordering patterns
* Track sales trends across time
* Improve average order value and customer satisfaction

---

##  Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor key KPIs like Sales, Orders, and Ratings
* Identify growth opportunities across locations
* Understand category-wise demand (Veg vs Non-Veg)
* Analyze time-based trends (daily, weekly, monthly)
* Enable strategic, data-driven decisions

---

##  Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is designed in four analytical layers:

1. **KPI Layer** – Quick overview of performance
2. **Trend Analysis** – Monthly, weekly, and daily sales trends
3. **Category & Regional Insights** – Food preferences and location performance
4. **Detailed Insights** – Drill-down tables and comparisons

##  Insights & Analysis

###  Sales Trends

* Sales remain stable between ₹6.2M – ₹6.9M
* Dip observed in February with recovery in later months
* Weekly trends show consistent performance with minor spikes

 **Insight:** Indicates steady demand with seasonal variations

---

###  Daily Performance

* Highest Sales: Saturday (~₹7.78M)
* Lowest Sales: Tuesday (~₹7.36M)

 **Insight:** Weekends are peak revenue drivers

---

###  Customer Preferences

* Veg Orders: 65%
* Non-Veg Orders: 35%

 **Insight:** Strong preference for veg items

---

###  Regional Insights

* Top Performing City: Bengaluru (~₹5.46M)
* Other Key Cities: Lucknow, Hyderabad, Mumbai

 **Insight:** Both metro and tier-2 cities contribute significantly

---

###  Quarterly Performance

* Q1 & Q2 outperform Q3

 **Insight:** Possible seasonal or campaign-driven impact

---

##  Key Learnings

*  Weekend demand drives higher revenue
*  Veg category dominates customer orders
*  Certain cities significantly outperform others
*  Stable ratings indicate consistent customer satisfaction
*  Seasonal dips highlight opportunities for targeted campaigns
